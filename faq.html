FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What distinguishes cold email outreach from email marketing?

Cold email outreach involves initiating contact with individuals you don’t yet know, akin to making a cold call but via email. This approach uses specific mailboxes to reach out to potential prospects.

Email marketing, on the other hand, involves sending emails to recipients who have opted into your subscription list. These can include newsletters, receipts, reminders, etc., typically sent using platforms like Brevo or Klaviyo.

Our process is designed to comply with the Data Protection Act of 2019, CASL, GDPR, and CCPA.
