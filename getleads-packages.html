FAQ

1. What is this service about?

In this service, we generate a list of highly targeted prospects that have a high probability of converting into Marketing Qualified Leads (MQL) and Sales Qualified Leads (SQLs) that are also campaign-ready.

2. What should I expect from you in terms of deliverables?

Expect a clean, verified list of B2B prospects containing the following fields at a minimum:

- Company Name 
- Company Website
- Company Phone Number
- Company Address
- Contact First Name
- Contact Last Name
- Contact Job Title 
- Email Address (valid)
- LinkedIn URL

3. What methods do you use to compile the leads list?

We never buy data, we use a number of different tools to curate data sets that fit into your target audience. We like to call it the harpoon method because we are only going after prospects that fit.

4. Can I get a sample before I place an order?

Yes, we can provide up to 5 prospects. Send your request via email with the subject "Sample Leads".

5. Are your email lists verified?

Yes. Email addresses are obtained from the profiles of prospects so they're highly relevant but we also run our lists through email verification algorithms so they're double-verified just to be sure. Incase you enounter any bounces we'll replace the leads for free.

6. Can you provide C-Suite Contacts?

Yes, according to your specifications, we can provide you with C-level contacts (CEO, Owner, Founder, COO, CFO, etc.) or specific job title leads from any industries/locations.
